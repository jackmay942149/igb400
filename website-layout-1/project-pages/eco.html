Description

This project is an on-going project by QUT faculty of science aimed at promoting interest in STEM to students. For the portion I have been part of this larger project we were tasked with creating the 'Daintree' module, which is an educational experience learning the dangers of invasive weeds, identifying native plants, and how to better maintain the environment. We created a NPC guided experience which led through a variety of minigames all designed to educate and entertain the players.

Team Information

This was a collaborative effort that had 1 Programmer (myself), 2 Artists, 1 Producer and Several Liason Staff. There are other team members working on other modules however contributors the daintree experience has been listed.
