Description

This project was a collaborative effort with Breda University, Netherlands and Banijay Entertainment. We were tasked with utilising VR technology to modernise our choice of one of there renowned intellectual properties. We selected Masterchef and created a gauntlet style rendition of the tv show set in fantastical locations. The project had a fantastic reception with everyone involved and was showcased at the QUT VR Showcase in 2024 and QUT Open Day 2025.

Team Information

This was a collaborative effort that had many contributors. There were 2 Game Designers, 2 Game Programmers (including myself), 5 Marketing and Product Researches (Breda University Contributors), and 2 IP Consultants (Banijay Entertainment).
