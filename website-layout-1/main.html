Masterchef VR

Games VR C# Unity

Team Size: 4 + 5

Masterchef VR is a university project in collaboration with Banijay Entertainment and Breda University, in the Netherlands (the + 5). We made a VR experience to emulate what it would be like to be a contestant in Masterchef, with challenges only virtual reality could provide. This is one of my favourite projects working with Banijay on a very visible IP.

Rock Bottom

Games Mobile C++ Unreal

Team Size: 4

Rock Bottom is my first publically released game, available on the Android Play Store.
